Dear Editors,

We are pleased to submit the manuscript entitled **"AI-guided discovery of climate precursors for global vegetation stress"** for consideration as an Article in *Nature*.

This manuscript addresses a central challenge in climate and ecosystem science: identifying the climate states that precede vegetation stress before stress is visible in satellite vegetation anomalies. Using public reanalysis, satellite and eddy-covariance records, the study develops an interpretable artificial intelligence framework to discover compound heat-drought precursor states and test them through temporal holdout, spatial holdout, baseline comparison and independent ecosystem-flux validation.
