A shared-grid ecological informatics workflow aligns MODIS vegetation stress with ERA5-Land climate data (2000-2025).

Soil moisture and VPD dominate 16-32 day climate precursor signals across Australia.

XGBoost improves stress-event detection but climate-only models retain false-alarm ratios above 0.80.

Persistence baseline (F1=0.660) outperforms all climate-only predictors, demonstrating the need for vegetation-memory features.

The full pipeline is reproducible through GEE, Python, GitHub and Zenodo.
